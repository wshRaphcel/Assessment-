Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most common questions we receive:

  • What is your return policy?
    We accept returns within 30 days of purchase as long as the items are in new, unused condition.
  • How do I track my order?
    After placing an order, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your shipment on our website.
  • Do you offer international shipping?
    Yes, we offer international shipping to most countries. Shipping fees will vary depending on your location.
  • Can I exchange products?
    Yes, exchanges are allowed within 30 days of receiving your order, as long as the products are in new and unused condition.
